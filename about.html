33 years and counting!



EST. 1989

Image has been printing in Sydney since the late 80's. Our first piece of the equipment was the Macintosh Classic.

Many things have changed over the decades and we've continued to deliver high quality products and service to our loyal clients.

WHY WE'RE DIFFERENT

We have a complete knowledge of prepress and postpress we offer an up to date knowledge of a wide variety of printing possibilities.

With in-house digital printing using the latest Xerox machines and established relationships with larger offset printing companies, we can supply anything from business cards to AO posters.

We pride ourselves at our attention to detail and always recommend the most economical way of completing a job.

We are a small team that takes advantage of the big printing companies when necessary, taking full responsibility for the final product. You always deal with the owners at Image, enabling quick replies to any issues or queries you might have.
